**TermCardModule.js**
Purpose:
Responsible for rendering term cards visually in a timeline layout.

Responsibilities:
- Renders all term cards, grouped into non-overlapping rows.
- Calculates dynamic widths and positions based on term durations.
- Ensures timeline alignment (e.g., 2x 6mo = 1x 12mo).
- Attaches edit/delete handlers and emits events accordingly.
